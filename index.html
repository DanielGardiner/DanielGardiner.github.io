ABOUT ME

I am currently working for Nudge Digital, a digital agency based in Bristol. I have had the opportunity to worked on a mix of greenfield projects and legacy code working within an agile team where I have actively contributed towards sprint planning, reviews and retrospectives. I have worked on two new build projects using React and have provided support to fourteen different Drupal sites.

I completed the Mayden Academy Full Stack Training Course, a 4 month intesive training course industry using industry standard methodologies such as OOP, SOLID principles, design patterns, frameworks, unit testing, git and task automation managers. I gained experience acting as a scrum master and gained Scrum Master Certification from the Scrum Alliance

Prior to starting the Mayden Academy Training Course I worked as a Senior Infectious Disease Epidemiologist for Public Health England, where I worked for 7 years. This involved investigating disease outbreaks, conducting research studies, and working extensively with R/RStudio to manipulate and analyse data.

I have also worked as a Consultant for the World Health Organization automating the production of influenza surveillance reports for over 50 European Regions using R/RStudio.

Career Change Motivation

Whilst working as an epidemiologist I would actively seek out as many opportunities as possible involving writing code, problem solving and building useful tools. However, I had many non-tech related responsibilities which would draw me away from the thing I enjoyed doing most (writing code!). I therefore decided to take the plunge and change career in order to make the things I'm passionate about the focus of my daily activities.
